About Me

The idea behind my website is to offer a simple and easy way to locate recipes for both meals and drinks. In addition, I just hope to use this as a personal catalog for my own recipes, so that I can share my recipes with friends and family and access the recipes I frequent at any time and at any place. I hope to include recipes that are fairly simple, and those that may be more complicated and may make use of more exotic ingredients. I also want to keep this site very simple and easy to access rather than obfuscating its purpose by including unnecessary details and background behind the dish, and instead providing the recipe straight away, and including the notes below that go into detail about possible substitutions and adjustments that can be made to either change the flavor, texture, or accessibility of the dish to certain diets.
